Man's chief aim in life is to be happy.
His progress and failure depend on the method he uses in
securing that happiness.
Happiness is within the reach of every individual.
One reason why man feels that happiness is impossible is
because he does not know the true meaning of such a state for
by experience we are told that as soon as the meaning of happy
ness becomes clear to man he falls under its blessedness.
What is happiness?
It is not contentment nor pleasure. For contentment is often
a fulfilled desire which envolves persons and things, and pleasure
is a gratification of the sense which can be purchased with money.
The more money man has for buying pleasures the poorer he is.
This is happiness: A state of being characterized by a certain
inward emotion which brings all outside forces in harmony with
itself. It is a mental state depending on the adjustment of the
individual's self consciousness and its relation with the scheme of
all things created here and above. It is therefore a result indepen
dent of. all material things and persons.
Contentment stifles gowth. It is self satisfying: a comfortable
home, business well started, enough money to live a hundred
years why go on? '
Happiness is the sunshine for mental growth; It is strength
ened only by virtue.

As a theoretical proposal compulsory automobile liability in
urance seemed to offer a remedv for the errowiner Droblem of
eckless driving, and irresponsability of drivers.
In practice such insurance has apparently become a burden,
egally and financially, on the community, without accomplishing
ts purpose. '
In Massachusetts, where this form of insurance is being tried,
.he results have been questionable.
Unscrupulous individuals have faked claims. Juries taking the
narrow and false viewpoint that only the insurance companies pay
the bills, have handed in excessive and utterly unjustified verdicts.
In a number of important forms of accidents, especially those
where the injured party can be proven anywhere at fault, liability
i? not possible.
Insurance companies are forced to take undesirable risks that
have resulted in their paying out more money than they take in.
As a consequence, rates must be raised and the many careful
drivers must pay for the damage caused by th reckless or -responsible
drivers. "
In Massachusetts, accidents are as prevalent as ever and in
ome communities more so. Reckless driving is said to be on the
nerease because wholesale Insurance relieves the individual of
ponsabililty.

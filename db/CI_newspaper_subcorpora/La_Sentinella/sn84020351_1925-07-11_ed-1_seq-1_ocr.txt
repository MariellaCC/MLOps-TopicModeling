SATURDAY NIGHT PARKING
Bridgeport like every other city does not have sufficient
parking space for automobiles at times when there are numerous
persons in the downtown district. This is especially true of Sat
urday nights.
Last Saturday night the streets were badly congested with
automobiles and parking pace was at a premium, so many were
the number of people who came to the city for the evening.
Bridgeport residents can help materially to relieve this con
gestion by leaving their own automobiles at home. It would be
comparatively esy for Bridgeport people to walk down town on
Saturday night. If this is found inconvenient it would be well
for those who ride to have their autos returned to the home
garage so there would be available for general use as much-parking
space as possible".
Some city residents park their autos in the business district
on Saturday night and thereby close the spaces to those who
come here from distant places. It is a severe handicap to out of
town traders to find parking space at a premium. The retail busi
ness district should be kept as free as possible of Bridgeort cars
on Saturday night. There will -be enough non-residents here to
take up all thy space available in the business area and it is a
matter of courtesy and hospitality to give them the right of way
for -that night.
Leave your own car in the garage on Saturday night if you
reside in Bridgeport, or else park it out of the business district.
Try the plan a while and sec how well it works and how much
comfort it will add to the traders' trip to your town. -

in the kitchen, it seems to have a wonderful tonic effect on

vided with a full basket of eatables. The men are .not always

did not have a1 hot dinner, yet out on the picnic the cold boiled
eggs and bread and jam go down as food fit for the gods.

led along in the old rut awhile, almost enythmg that looks dif
ferent looks god. However, natural beauty stirs a feeling of ro
mance, which covers even prosaic food with its haze of .illusion.

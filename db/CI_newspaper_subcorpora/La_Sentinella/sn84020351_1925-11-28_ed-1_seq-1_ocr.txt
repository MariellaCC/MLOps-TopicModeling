THE TRAFFIC POBLEM
The American Engineering Council, composed of thirty
national and local societies, is studying the traffic problem.
The traffic problem in some form or another is acute in every
city in the United States.
It has been handled long enough by politicians. It is time
the engineers were' taking hold, and it is hoped the project will
have some results.

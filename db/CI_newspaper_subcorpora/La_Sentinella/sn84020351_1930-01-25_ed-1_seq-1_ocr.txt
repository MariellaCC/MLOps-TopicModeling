HIS PERSONALITY
Study one of his photographs; his bold features are known
to everyone. Piercing eyes, a rugged jaw. a defiant demeanor,
an equiline nose ; a master at posing and withal an imposing
political genius !
See him in action : Observe how he sways the mass. Yes,
within that mind there is the determination to exalt Italy; to
blaze the trail in industry and to lead his extraordinary people
to their traditional splendor!
THE RISE OF FASCISM!
Fascism is no longer a political experiment; it is now the
embodiment of the spirit of the Italian people. It is indeed
triumphant. But behind this political exuberance is the man
BENITO MUSSOLINI!
Here are the facts : In the general election, held a short time
ago, the Fascists received all but 136,000 votes out of 8,650,000
cast! The press of every nation heralded this event as a sens
ational victory for Mussolini !
Surely, this vote of confidence displays the sentiment of the
Italians. We are flourishing in the age of Mussolini!
ACCOMPLISHMENTS
Mussolini has assumed the role of a constructive states
man ; he is now engaged in developing the industries and resources
of his country.
Another Mussolini triumph was the settlement of a -dispute
which has challenged the efforts of other Popes .and Italian
statesmen for nearly sixty years. I am referring to the Roman
question. Surely, Il Duce displayed brilliant statesmanship-in this
matter. In fact, prominent historians consider this diplomatic
stroke one of the most important political achievements in history!
Many years have passed since Mussolini assumed control
of Italian affairs. What have been his accomplishments? Every
where there is social and economic stability ; the chaos and
discordant struggle of parties and personalities have vanished ;
religion sways the soul once more; the spirit of progress rules;
education is triumphant; pessimism and skepticism are crushed
and optimism emerges; yes, everywhere one hears a beautiful
symphony Fascism! This political philosophy is based upon
the great principles of life; it represents the will of the Italian
people!

wnership of industries and utilities. : , . ;
Certainly, if Russia under the Soviet regime had "panned out"
ven fairly successfully it would be a tremendous and definite
oost for paternalistic government. But recent disclosures and

ment doing everything, instead of the individual, has resulted in

nycal of autocracies. Freedom of speech and press is unknown.

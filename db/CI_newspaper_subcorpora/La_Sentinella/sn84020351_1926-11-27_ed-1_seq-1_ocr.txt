THE MADNESS OF BIGOTS
Professor Bruno Roselli of Vassar is having a taste of aggresr
sive bigotry. Within the past few weeks he has received a number
of anonymous letters asking him to leave the country. He has
been bitterly denounced in the columns of the official organ of the
Ku Klux Klan. And a demand has been made on the President of
Vassar College asking that the Professor be discharged.
All this because in the course of an address delivered in
Poughkeepsie on Columbus Day Professor Roselli dared criticise
our present immigration law. We are quite sure that Mr. Roselli
will not be bullied by the stupid .and cowardly methods of his
enemies, and that, as a war veteran who has tasted more bitter
attacks than these; he will take care of himself should the occasion
demand it. We are sure, furthemore, that Vassar College will not
bow to the will of the bigots of Poughkeepsie. But Professor Ro
selli 's case, typical of many others throughout the land, should be
throroughly investigated. The community of Poughkeepsie ought
not allow so high-handed an incident to pass without reacting.

THE SPIRIT OF REVOLT
Let us study the mighty currents which mould
and transform civilization!
The Stage of Life!
By Arthur Cortese, B. S. C.
INTRODUCTION
We are flourishing in a wonderful age; superpower sways
human conduct and materialism dominates the soul. There, is rest
lessness here and there; India dashes away from traditional
moorings and the mighty British Empire is gradually losing its
glamour in a flux of world powers!
Gandhi revolts and leads his people into the channels of wis
dom He is imprisoned and now haughty India sinks to its original
state of barbarism. India a mosaic of conflicting egos, yet
luring and intriguing, covers an area of 1,800,000 square miles.
Population 320,000,000 souls! What a vassal what savagery!
It was Gandhi's ambition to arouse the patriotism of his people
but the barbarism of the ages impeded his efforts. Here is a geo
grtii hical spot where all races intermingle: 220,000,000 Hindus.
70,000,000 Mohammedans, 12,000,000 Buddhists, 5,000,000 mem
bers of various tribes and 3,500,000 strutting Sikhs! Shakespeare
was right: the world is a stage and we are the actors! Did he
include India?
FEUDALISM
Feudalism, the relationship of lord and serf, persisted for
centuries; but man revolted and destroyed this selfish institution.
But in India Feudalism is still triumphant! Can you picture the
Hindus as they march through the streets, drums roaring and
banners swaying to and fro in the breeze? Yes, they are happy.
But man does not progress by happiness alone!
Meanwhile, the West is advancing in every way mechan
isms, wholesale mass education, babels of finance, dashing
airplanes, naval conferences. Energy shot from titanic stations
supply electricity everywhere, and withal, there is a lure of
progress. The East lags while the West leaps!
MEANING OF PROGRESS
But are we really progressing? Materially yes! Spiritually?
We dare not say! The Roman Empire command all men. It was
strong, powerful, dynamic and vast! But what caused its crash?
Was it the invading avalanche of terror ? No ! Was it bribery ? No !
What was then? It was a malicious, wilful and deliberate disease
degeneration!
Rome did not collapse in a day; it was hundreds of years
before the ravages of degeneration poisoned that noble empire!
Rome constructed extraordinary roads but of what good were
these roads if the youth of the nation proclaimed their sins with
revelry? Imperial Rome carved upon the rock of time great laws"
of human conduct, but of what value were these laws if the
family was crumbling?
SCENES TODAY
A spirit of revolt animates all men ! Even ancient institutions
are cracking under a pressure of change. Marriage is decaying,
materialism gains momentum while religion dies. Gold displaces
God, and the family is disintegrating. Everything is new!
Events follow teach other in rapid succession; trends of
thought revolutionize culture; governments rise and fall; person
alities emerge and dwindle; religious fads rule the day and chaos
is king! Everything SEEMS to be in order but in reality there is
everywhere the triumph of decay ! Look into the hearts of men
and what do you see ? Gold ! Gold ! Yes, the soul has been disrupted
by industry, and machinery echoes its eternal babble of war and
despair, strife and confusion, and misery and conflagration! No
wonder Oswald Spengler, the giant thinker of our age, has
announced the Decline of the West!
WHAT IS PROGRESS?
Does progress consist of steel and stones? Does progress
signify the victory of the mob ? What is progress ? Is a towering
skyscraper the symbol of progress? It is a very difficult word to
define because life is so complex and mobile that it upsets any
definition with a million exceptions. However, history teaches us
that material prosperity is not of itself progress; to be progress
.here must be some other guidance! Greece had constructed
nighty edifices of art but it too flopped into sea of obscurity.
vVliy? Because a nation without God crumbles!
Russia has not learned this lesson; its catastrophe is
inevitable ! And so in our age, with all of its power and superpower,
there is decadence. Religion has lost its grip on human society
and that is a sign of impending peril! Theism and atheism clash;
Humanism proudly emerges with a system of morality and discards
the dogmas of the past.
Science reduces everything to mathematical laws; philosophy
expounds pessimism with glee; Nietzsche, Schopenhauer, and
Leopardi arise again from their eternal slumber and denounce the
mass; Diogenes roams the streets in a new garb and Hegesius
recommends suicide. What an age!
The Biologist tells us, without emotion and irritation, that
death is merely a chemical change. He informs us politely that
when you cut off the head of a chicken its body still runs around
cut off a frog's head and the body jumps a turtle's heart
may be removed after the turtle has met death and the heart
continues to beat for a week!
These things frighten us and we boldly yet timidly fluctate
between life and death! Atheism conqueres us and we deflate the
?;mse of the causes of things! Degeneration ensues and civilization
noves into the jungles!
SOLUTION
These statements are not generalities. History substantiates
;kem and shows that civilization is a series of births and funerals ;
liat it rises when God rules and falls when religion wanes!
Let us not be impressed with those soaring massive steel
nonuments which darken our streets. Progress does not consist
)f steel and stones; think of Egypt and its pyramids!
Remember that when the soul is disrupted society
disintegrates regardless of other material achievements. Let us
xo into the gieat open spaces with spiritual exuberance! And
emember that a nation without God must perish!

he has recently delivered to the Modern Churchmen's Union oc

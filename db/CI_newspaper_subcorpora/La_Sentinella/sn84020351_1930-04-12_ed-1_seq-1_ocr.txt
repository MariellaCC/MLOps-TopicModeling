HOW TO SLEEP
A health writer gives a few sensible rules for sleeping which
may be boiled down as follows :
Go to bed expecting to sleep, not to think'.
Retire at regular time, thus forming a habit which promotes
sleep.
Don't read or study in bed the mind should stop working
Face away from the light, especially as summer comes on.
See that bedroom is cool before turning in. .
See that you are covered well enough to keep warm.
To. these may be added the following practical suggestion If
you still have difficulty in getting to sleep, take a short walk
before going to bed. Sleeplessness is often the result of too little
exercise and fresh air.

OPTIMISTIC NOTE
Who ever started talking about hard times anyway? We have
heard it all our lives and it never did amount to anything. It never
did get you anywhere. You will hear about as much . about hard
times in the very. midst of prosperity as you will when things do
not look quite 30 bright and promising. There appears no cause
whatever why any one in the Andalusia trade territory should
cry hard times, yet you will hear it occasionally. Everybody haj
got plenty to eat, many with money on deposit drawing interest
and everybody with a job that wants one. Yet in the very face
of these unquestionable .facts you will occasionally "hear some
guy holler it sure is hard times. So often; we talk when we know
not what we are talking about and could not if called upon, give
one single plausible reason that would justify the remark.

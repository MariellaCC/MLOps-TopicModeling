DO YOUR WINDOW SHOPPING EARLY.
If you haven't taken a trip down town afoot lately, there
will be no better time than tonight. Rouse yourself from the
customary fireside reveries of evening, crank up thè old bus
(more probably -it's a newi one) and steer her towards town.
Then park somewhere ciose in and put in an hour doing window
shopping. It's a great sport; inexpensive and instructive. Christ
mas is only a few days away, and you can select all your gifts
this way, at your leisure ; then, go back tomorrow and buy them
pronto.
Bridgeport shop windows are now crowded with the choic
est merchandise the world affords; the carefully selected pur
chases that local merchandise buyers perspired over in 'August
have arrived and been placed in settings that are a delight to the
eye. They are in a range that suits all needs and at prices that
positively would make a pocketbook chuckle with glee.

ping Early"; afterwards, take the family to the show; and, to
morrow, go back and ask for your selections of tonight. Thus
you will save time, and know, for once, at least, the satisfaction
of having everything ready long before the great day of giving.

BETTER FIRST LOOK AROUND YOU.
What can possess a man that he pulls up stakes in this
choice region and starts out for the end of the Florida rainbow?
We do not inveigh against that sunny state's seductive charms;
and, anyway, this is a free country in which a man can go where
he pleases. But how anybody can be blind to the opportunities
lying here in Connecticut while he visions in perfect clarity a
faraway land apparently bedight in milk and honey and gold,
is beyond our humble comprehension.
Go on to Florida if you .will; but remember this : The pros
perity in Florida is being TAKEN there from-the outside; it is
not coming from within. 'And only that wealth which can and
does come out of the state will regulate its commercial pros
perity, once the condition of inflation dies out.
BRINGING JQY .TO OTHERS
Let our Christmas celebration this year be truly Christ-like,
let us begin our worship with rhe merry day, and continue it
throughout the year, not giving in material things alone, but
giving of self and service.
if you would know the Merriest Christmas of a life time, go
personally and search, not along the highways out in the byways,
not that you may give money but that you may personally go to
the manager of love and service, there finding those who are
truly worthy, but not of the class who are reached by the many
well-known agencies for such work. When the truly worthy
overlooked and underprivileged ones are found, let Christmas
joy be given through the full basket, toys and Christmas party,
but, best of all, assure them of a personal interest which shall not
be for a day only.

selves going up the stream of success so danged fast that the
barnacles of failure wouldn't have a chance in the world to hook

CROSSINGS MORE NUMEROUS?
Railroad grade crossings are becoming more numerous
every year instead of being reduced, according to a railroad man
in an address last week. He said that in an average year while
T05 old grade crossings are being eliminated 4,437 are being
built, the volume of increases being several times greater .than
the reduction. The grade crossing, is one of the greatest prob
lems before the American public today, and the man who can
find a solution of it will render a great service to humanity. The
tremendous cost makes it almost impossible to eliminate all of
them.

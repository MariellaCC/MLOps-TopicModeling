the cheerful booster as he walks down to his work. "Seems to me
the lawns never looked so smooth, nor the flowers so bright. And
people are keeping up their places better than ever before, and a

going ahead, that he could do more business, and also improve his

A REMEDY FOR CROSSING ACCIDENTS
Prevention of grade crossing accidents is so easy and simple
that there is no excuse for such accidents. All that is necessary
is to look in both directions to see whether a train is approaching.
If a train is approaching, stop and let it go by.
When there is more than one track, wait after a train has
passed to make sure that another train is not appoaching on one
of the other tracks.
Where a crrossing does not afford good vision because of view
being obstructed by building, trees, curves or embankments, stop,
look and listen before crossing.
When signals are in action, crossing gates lowered or flagman
is at crossing, train is approching and driver should stop and let
it pass.
These are reasonable regulations. If observed they will pre
vent grade crossing accidents just as effectively as grade changes.
When not observed, the driver's license should be cancelled.
In other words, by such regulations, crossing accidents can be
averted without loading up municipalities with taxes for grade
changes.

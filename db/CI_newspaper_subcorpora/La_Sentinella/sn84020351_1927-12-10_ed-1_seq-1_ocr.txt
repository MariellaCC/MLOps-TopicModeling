TOWN BOOSTERS

shouting for their respective communities, and trying to interest
people to move into them and start business enterprises there.
Some critical folks think this boosting is exaggereated, and that
the towns that are so extolled do not come up to the description.

boosting a city, they do a lot of things that help its substantial
progress. When they boast that a city has advantages, they feel
it necessary to make good on their claim. They spreed abroad a
feeling of hope and confidence, and when people have that senti
ment, they do more business, they improve the community, and
the whole place rises to a new level of development:

is a lot of bunk. It's fine for the kids, of course. But it's about

easy ; set you to wondering if we, in this speedy twentieth century,

Let's look into. How about it anyway: is Christmas "a lot

after all, that alone is plenty of excuse for continuing it. We

life? It is not. The more complex and hurried life becomes, the
more does each man retain, jealously and ardently, that little shy
spark of child-like faith and hope and freshness in his heart.' If
that is not the case, why is it that little Willie's father always
manages to play with Willie'sméw train pf cars about three times

been arid never will be commercialized. That is the spirit of kind
ness and unselfishness that the day brings. It is something we
need desperately. Once a year is not too often for us to forget
about ourselves and think of others. It is not too often for us to
remember that there are such things as neighborliness. and
Christian self -forgetfulness. .. :
